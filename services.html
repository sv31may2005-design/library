Library Services

We provide a range of academic and technical services to support the learning needs of students and faculty.

Core Facilities

Lending Service

Automated issue and return of books using RFID technology. Students can borrow up to 4 books for 14 days.

Reference Section

A dedicated quiet zone containing encyclopedias, dictionaries, yearbooks, and rare manuscripts for in-library use.

Reprography

On-site photocopying and printing services are available for students to copy research papers and syllabus materials.

Wi-Fi & Internet

High-speed campus Wi-Fi access in the reading halls and a dedicated computer lab for academic research.

Periodicals

Subscription to 15+ national newspapers and 40+ academic magazines for competitive exam preparation.

Career Corner

Special collection of books for UPSC, HPAS, UGC-NET, and other competitive examinations.

N-LIST & Digital Access

Our library is a member of the N-LIST (National Library and Information Services Infrastructure for Scholarly Content) program, providing access to e-resources to students and faculty.

  • 6,000+ e-journals
  • 3,00,000+ e-books
  • Remote access login available
Access N-LIST

Book Bank Scheme

We believe financial constraints should not hinder education. The Book Bank facility allows deserving and meritorious students to borrow textbooks for the entire semester.

  • Available for SC/ST/BPL students
  • Keep books for full semester
  • Minimal maintenance fee
